Do you want to meet your teacher?

Brandon Ferguson

Hello! My name is Brandon, and I've been teaching English to hard-working students and professionals for more than ten years.

I actually started out as a Spanish teacher in the United States, where I'm originally from. In 2004, I moved to Madrid, where I ran a small but very successful exam preparation school, and since 2010, I've been teaching one-to-one lessons here in Dublin. My focus is primarily on Cambridge exam preparation (FCE and CAE), IELTS preparation, and helping international students and foreign professionals perfect their English through personalised coaching. I have significant experience working speakers of Spanish, Italian, Arabic, Korean, Chinese, and Polish.

The reason I became an English teacher is because I enjoy helping people expand their vocabulary, improve their pronunciation, correct errors, and sharpen their writing skills. Overall, my goal is to help you become more fluent and more confident speaking English.

I’d love to help you, so please get in touch today and we can arrange a time for a chat!
