Where the lessons are held

I give lessons from my home on Hanover Quay, which is conveniently located in beautiful Grand Canal Square, Dublin 2, just a stone’s throw from the European headquarters of Facebook and Google. When we have set a date for the level test, I will send you an email with the exact address and detailed directions. If you prefer, I can travel to your home or office; depending on the distance, a travel fee will be charged.

Getting here

Grand Canal Square is very easy to get to. It's about 1.5 km east of the main entrance to Trinity College and is served by six Dublin Bus routes. There are DART and LUAS stations nearby.

  • Dublin Bus: routes 1, 47, 56a, and 77a. These buses all go down Pearse Street. If you're coming from the city centre, get off at Grand Canal Quay, stop 353; if you're coming from Sandymount, get off at stop 396
  • Dublin Bus: routes 15a or 15b. But NOT route 15! These buses go from Rathmines through the city centre and along the north quays. Get off at the very last stop, Benson Street, stop 7077
  • DART to Grand Canal Dock Station, then walk north to Grand Canal Square
  • LUAS Red Line to Mayor Square/NCI, then cross the Samuel Beckett Bridge
