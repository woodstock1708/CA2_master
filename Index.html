What can Elephant English do for you?

Exam Preparation in Dublin

The one-to-one exam prep courses I offer are tailor-made for your specific needs and timetable. I have experience teaching Cambridge First (FCE), Cambridge Advanced (CAE), and IELTS, and I know how important passing these exams is, and how stressful preparing can be.

++ Ready for learning English?

General English in Dublin

We will work together to strengthen all skills areas --speaking, writing, vocabulary and grammar, listening and reading-- and you will leave feeling more confident and fluent. These classes are fun yet demanding and provide an alternative to time-consuming group classes.

++ Ready for progressing with your English?

Distance Writing Workshops

I love helping students become better writers is my true speciality. What I’ve found as both a language learner and teacher is that students generally do not get enough writing practice in group classes, and they don’t get enough construction criticism from their teachers.

++ Improve your English writing skills now!

Do you want to meet Elephant English?

I am Brandon Ferguson

I’ve always loved learning languages, ever since I took Latin in high school. I speak fluent Spanish and some Italian, and from personal experience, I know how being proficient in a second language can open doors, so it only made sense for me to take what I love and make a career out of it!

My main interests as a teacher lie in find new ways to help students expand their vocabulary, improve their pronunciation, correct errors, and hone their writing skills. Overall, my goal is to help you become more fluent and more confident speaking English.
