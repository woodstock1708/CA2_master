Prices and Payment

Pricing Breakdown

01. Prices for Exam Preparation Courses

The amount of time you will need to prepare for Cambridge FCE, CAE or IELTS depends on four factors: your level when you start the classes, when your exam is, what mark you want to achieve, and most of all, how hard you are willing to work, both in and outside of class.

Students who already have a very high level and an excellent work ethic can sometimes do it in 10 hours, but I've found that the average student needs at least 30 hours of lessons. I will be very honest with you when you begin and tell you what I think you'll need, so there will be no surprises down the road. You can start off by booking a five-class course, and then decide how you want to continue from there.

Price for one exam preparation course:

  • One course (five classes / ten hours): 225 €

02. Prices for General English lessons

Most students like to book one or two classes a week, and 90 minutes is usually the right amount of time for most people.

Every month we will decide on our schedule for the month, and then I will bill you for those classes in advance, just like a course. I offer a 10% discount for students who book two or more classes per week, and I can offer group discounts if you want to work with a friend or two. Just ask!

Prices for one-to-one general English lessons:

  • One hour: 30 € (express option)
  • 90 minutes: 35 € (most popular option)
  • Two hours: 45 € (best value for money)

03. Prices for Distance Writing Workshops

You will have access to all my writing resources, including excellent models, and I will carefully correct your errors and offer you meaningful criticism and suggestions for improvement. You will then rewrite your piece and resubmit it, and we'll keep doing that until we're satisfied. And then you'll do that nine more times! Before we start working together, we will sign a contract with a start and end date for the workshop. Most students need about three months to complete all ten pieces.

Prices for distance writing workshops:

  • Cambridge FCE: 10 pieces of writing - 200€
  • Cambridge CAE: 10 pieces of writing - 275€)
  • IELTS Academic: 10 pieces of writing - 250€
  • TOEFL iBT: 10 pieces of writing - 250€

Invoicing and Cancellation

Invoicing

Exam prep students will receive an invoice at the beginning of their five-class course; General English students will receive an invoice at the beginning of every month. Payment is made through bank transfer.

Students doing distance writing workshops or CVs will receive an invoice in advance and can send payment via PayPal. There is no VAT charge on classes.

Cancellation Policy

Sometimes life gets in the way, and you need to cancel your class – I totally understand. If that happens, text me the night before, and we will reschedule within the billing cycle, depending on my availability. Unfortunately I cannot accommodate same-day cancellations. Cancelled classes cannot be rolled over from one billing cycle to the next. They must be made up within the billing cycle.
