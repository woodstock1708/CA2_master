What can Elephant English do for you?

01. Exam Preparation in Dublin

The one-to-one exam prep courses I offer are tailor-made for your specific needs and timetable. I have many years of experience teaching Cambridge First (FCE), Cambridge Advanced (CAE), and IELTS, and I understand how important passing these exams is, and how stressful preparing for them can be.

Exam coaching classes focus on specific test modules, and we’ll use past papers as well as materials from other books (and my own original materials). This method pays special attention to the skills and strategies you'll need to get a top score on your exam. Of course, you will improve your general spoken and written English --and your confidence-- at the same time.

I’ve created some helpful FAQs about the exams that I teach. Just click on the test you’re planning to take and download the PDF.

02. General English in Dublin

In addition to my exam prep classes, I also offer private, one-to-one English lessons for motivated students and busy professionals. Elephant English students who opt for General English typically fall into one of three groups:

  • Working professionals who already have reached a more advanced level of English but feel they need to perfect their writing and speaking for their job
  • Partners of foreign working professionals who need to improve their English to be able to talk with their children’s teachers, doctors, etc.
  • Foreign students enrolled in a Dublin language school who want a more personalized experience in addition to their daily group classes at school

Lessons centre on topics that are interesting to you. We will work together to strengthen all skills areas --speaking, writing, vocabulary and grammar, listening and reading-- and you will leave feeling more confident and fluent. These classes are fun yet demanding and provide an alternative to time-consuming group classes.

03. Distance Writing Workshops

While I love all kinds of teaching, helping students become better writers is my true speciality. What I’ve found as both a language learner and teacher is that students generally do not get enough writing practice in group classes, and they don’t get enough construction criticism (feedback in the form corrections and suggestions) from their teachers. And even if they do, lots of times students don’t know what to do with that feedback.

Following the usual method of brainstorming, planning, writing and revising really isn’t enough to do well. To become a good writer, especially at CAE and IELTS, you need to see models of what is expected, and you need to formulate your response with those models in mind. And then you need to practice writing in that style, and rewriting and refining your work until it's exactly what the FCE, CAE and IELTS examiners are looking for.

So in early 2015, I will start offering writing courses with constructive criticism for students all over the world. Whether you need a complete writing course for FCE, CAE or IELTS, or just want to have someone give you a constructive critique on writing you’ve already done, you can turn to Elephant English for help. I'll be giving these course through a mixture of face-to-face lessons on Skype or Facetime with a lot of e-mail communication as well.
